About me

I received the B.S. degree from Tongji University, Shanghai, in 2020, and the M.S. degree from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2022. I am currently a Ph.D. student in Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. My main research interest is the behavior and long-term fate of waste in landfills, especially plastics. I also work on estimation and mitigation of greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions from solid waste disposal sites (SWDS).

Research

My research interests include:

  • Waste management

  • Plastic waste

  • Climate change
